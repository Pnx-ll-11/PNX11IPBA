About the Olmec Civilization

The Olmecs were the earliest known major Mesoamerican civilization.. They lived in the present-day Mexican states of Veracruz and Tabasco, in the lowland tropics on the Gulf of Mexico The Olmec, who had first emerged around 1600 BCE, were among the first Mesoamerican complex societies, and their culture influenced many later civilizations, including the Maya. The Olmec are best known for their massive stone heads carved from basalt, a volcanic rock. The Olmec civilization are the ones that fathom the concept of 0 and they created the first conduit system.
